Kinsey Wolanski was born August 30, 1996 in Sacramento, California. Russian-American. She started modeling early — Sports Illustrated and Maxim features before the world knew her name. Then came June 1, 2019.

Madrid. Wanda Metropolitano Stadium. Liverpool vs Tottenham. 63,000 spectators in the ground. Hundreds of millions watching globally. She sprinted onto the pitch during the Champions League Final in a black swimsuit. Was detained by security. Fined €5,000 by UEFA. And gained 2.3 million Instagram followers in 18 hours. "I really did this just because I really want to push myself out of my comfort zone. I love that adrenaline feeling."

"Getting my pilot's license was one of the most challenging but rewarding things I have ever done. I flew 112 hours before taking my flight check ride exam."
Kinsey Wolanski · @kinsey · Sports Illustrated · Maxim

Instead of riding the wave, she earned her dual-rated pilot license — certified to fly both fixed-wing aircraft and helicopters. 18 months of training. Three exams. 112 flight hours. She became a certified skydiver. A professionally listed stuntwoman. A sports host. A real estate investor with an agent's license. The moment in Madrid was the beginning of a completely different altitude.
